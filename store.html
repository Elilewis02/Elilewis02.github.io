Bringing People Together History

Founded in the mid-1980s, Champs Sports has grown from a single store to a major athletic footwear, apparel, and accessories retailer, now boasting over 540 locations across the United States, Canada, Puerto Rico, and the U.S. Virgin Islands. As a subsidiary of Foot Locker, Inc., Champs Sports is renowned for offering a wide range of products from leading brands like Nike, Adidas, and Under Armour. The company has also leveraged celebrity endorsements to enhance its brand appeal, collaborating with athletes and public figures to connect with a broader audience and stay at the forefront of sports and lifestyle fashion trends.
